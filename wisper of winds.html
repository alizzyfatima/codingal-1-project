Welcome to Wispering Winds Academy,where students learn all about dragons and how to care for them. Located high in the Elder Mountains, our school is a special place where kids and dragons become best friends. Here, students get to ride dragons, understand their behavior, and train with them in fun activities. Our expert teachers, who are experienced dragon riders, guide students through exciting lessons and adventures. .At Wispering Winds Academy every student discovers the magic of dragons.
