  • 🍵INGREDIANTS:

    Tea leaves or a tea bag (your choice of black, green, herbal, etc.) Water Optional: Milk, sugar, honey, lemon, or any other preferred additions
  • 🍵Instructions: Boil Water: Start by boiling fresh water. The quality of water can affect the taste of your tea, so try to use filtered water if possible. Warm the Teapot/Cup: If youre using a teapot, pour a little hot water into it to warm it up, then discard the water. This helps maintain the brewing temperature.
  • Add Tea: For Tea Bags: Place the tea bag in your cup or teapot. For Loose Leaf Tea: Measure about 1 teaspoon of tea leaves per cup. Place the tea leaves in an infuser or directly in the teapot.
  • 🍵Pour Hot Water: Pour the boiling water over the tea bag or leaves. Black Tea: Use boiling water (around 100°C or 212°F). Green Tea: Use slightly cooler water (around 80-85°C or 176-185°F). Herbal Tea: Use boiling water (around 100°C or 212°F).
  • 🍵Steep: Let the tea steep for the appropriate time:
    Black Tea: 3-5 minutes
    Green Tea: 2-3 minutes
    Herbal Tea: 5-7 minutes
  • 🍵Remove Tea Bag or Leaves: Remove the tea bag or strain the leaves out of the tea once it has steeped to the desired strength.
  • 🍵Add Optional Ingredients: Add milk, sugar, honey, lemon, or any other additions according to your taste preference.
  • 🍵Enjoy: Give it a good stir, let it cool to a comfortable temperature, and enjoy your delicious cup of tea!
